Who am I ?

Hi, My name is Andrijana, I’m 23 years old. I come from Smederevo in Serbia. I'm currently studying for a master's degree at Faculty of Technical Sciences majoring in Information Systems Engineering and currently looking for an internship for further career advancement. I'm person with a passion for exploring the IT world and so that different problem solving. The skills that make me effective in my career are my organized work schedule, my initiative to learn new technologies and my great curiosity to work with great ideas. 😀

More about me...

I like to challenge myself with a different job experience. I set real goals for myself, so I constantly have something to strive toward. My goal is to develop my critical thinking, increase my time management skills and learn new things throughout my work implementing them in real life, so that I'm always eager and energetic to learn new skills. I believe that continuous learning are the key factor for achieving personal and professional success. Just because of that, always keep keen on learning and staying current with new technologies. 💡

📣 I live by a very simple motto "Give your best in every thing you do, and it will lead to greater things in your future. Do that every day with consistency and see how far you can go in life". I always keep myself motivated and disciplined and improving myself to reach higher in my field. My core belief is that success is nothing but a few simple disciplines that are practiced every day without skipping.

I am very passionate and enthusiastic about creating constructive and efficient solutions. My greatest weakness is that I sometimes focus too much on making things perfect, although I know that perfectionists should practice being imperfect. Therefore, I am always guided by the familiar expression he says “Don’t let the perfect be the enemy of the good enough”. 📌

At the end of the day, the most important thing is to do the job I do because I love it. I like the IT industry.

While I'm working I like to listen to music from different artists because music does gives me positive energy to work better. In my spare time, i.e. when I am not coding, I enjoy socializing and going out for a cup of coffee. ☕ I like to research random topics, eat delicious foods, sleep and sing around the house, although I'm not good at it. 😆🎤🎶 Given that I spend most of my times in front of a laptop, in the free time, I really try to minimize the screen time. On weekends, I like to do short trips and I enjoy going for a walk the nature with my friends. I love to read books and I enjoy reading them, but I also believe that they help me to broaden my horizons, improve on my vocabulary and communication skills. 📖

I like to interact with people, listen to new ideas and I am always open to have talk about anything, so that look forward to connect with you. 🤝
